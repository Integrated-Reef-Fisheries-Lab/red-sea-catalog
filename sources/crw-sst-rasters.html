NOAA Coral Reef Watch Sea Surface Temperature Rasters — Red Sea

Daily 5 km sea surface temperature and thermal stress (Degree Heating Week) raster composites clipped to the Red Sea basin, derived from NOAA Coral Reef Watch satellite products. Used as the primary environmental baseline for bleaching risk and warming trend analyses.
