SAKMEO Reef Fish Visual Census Transect Data (2014-2023)

Belt-transect fish visual census records collected by SAKMEO field teams across Saudi Red Sea reef sites, spanning Farasan Islands in the south to sites near Jeddah/Thuwal in the central basin. Each row is a species/size-class observation on a transect (3x20 m or similar), with taxonomy (family/genus/species), size-class bins, counts, and derived biomass fields (total biomass per transect, biomass per m2, tonnes per hectare). Exported as a single flat worksheet from an internal KAUST-Aramco Phase II fish-data workbook (source path recorded in the file's embedded SharePoint metadata: '.../ARAMCO/PHASE II/Datasets/Fish data/').
