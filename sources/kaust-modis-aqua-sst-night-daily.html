AQUA/MODIS Sea Surface Temperature (Nighttime, 4 micron) Daily L3m — Red Sea

Daily NASA AQUA/MODIS Level-3 mapped (L3m) nighttime sea surface temperature granules (SST4 product, 4-micron shortwave-infrared retrieval, unaffected by daytime sun-glint/reflection) covering the Red Sea, archived as a directory of daily NetCDF granules, a tar bundle of the same granules, and a single time-merged 'combined' NetCDF file.
