Sea Around Us -- Reconstructed Catch Data, Northern and Central Red Sea Marine Ecoregion (MEOW)

Sea Around Us (SAU) reconstructed marine fisheries catch data aggregated to the 'Northern and Central Red Sea' Marine Ecoregion of the World (MEOW) unit, 1950-2019, spanning catches attributed to multiple fishing entities (e.g. Israel, Jordan, Saudi Arabia, Egypt) operating within that ecoregion rather than a single national EEZ. Same variable structure and methodology as the companion EEZ extract in this folder.

Known issues: Not a Red-Sea field survey -- spatial/statistical reference layer covering a whole MEOW ecoregion, not fully bounded within the site's Red Sea bbox (MEOW 30 also includes northern Gulf of Suez/Aqaba approaches); bbox above is an approximation.; Overlaps conceptually with sau-eez-saudi-redsea-catch-1950-2019 (same underlying SAU catch reconstruction, different spatial aggregation unit) -- avoid double-counting catch totals if combining the two.; A duplicate .zip archive also exists alongside it (SAU MEOW 30 v50-1.zip) -- not catalogued separately.
