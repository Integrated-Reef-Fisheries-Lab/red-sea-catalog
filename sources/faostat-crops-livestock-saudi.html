FAOSTAT — Crops and Livestock Products (QCL), Saudi Arabia, 1961–2024

FAO's Crops and Livestock Products (QCL) domain data filtered to Saudi Arabia, covering area harvested, production quantity, yield, live-animal stocks, and slaughter/laying statistics across 86 crop and livestock items, 1961–2024. Accompanied by FAO's full domain metadata (methodology, definitions, quality notes) and supporting PDFs on unit conversions and revision notes.

Known issues: FAOSTAT's own metadata reports imputation rates of 41% (2022), 45% (2023), and 52% (2024) for primary crop/livestock products, so a large and growing share of recent-year values are model-estimated rather than officially reported (see FAOSTAT_metadata.csv, section 14.3).; Limited to FAO's QCL (crops and livestock) domain — does not include FAO's fisheries/aquaculture statistics (FishStatJ), which the KAUST Sources registry lists separately as a '[Previous]' source but for which no local file was found in this folder.; National-level only; cannot be spatially attributed to the Red Sea coast versus the rest of the Kingdom.
