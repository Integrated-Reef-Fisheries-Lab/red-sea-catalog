Food Systems Dashboard — Saudi Arabia Country Profile Export

Cross-cutting food systems indicators for Saudi Arabia exported from the Food Systems Dashboard, spanning food environments, food supply chains, individual diets and nutrition outcomes, drivers (biophysical, socio-economic, political, innovation), and policies, compiled from multiple underlying sources (FAO, NCD-RisC, Beal et al., World Bank, etc.) with an accompanying metadata file defining each indicator's sector, source, unit, definition, and calculation method.
