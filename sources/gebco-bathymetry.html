GEBCO Global Bathymetry Grid — Red Sea Extent

Annual releases (2019-2025) of the GEBCO global terrain model, a continuous elevation/bathymetry grid at 15 arc-second resolution, clipped to the Red Sea and Gulf of Aden extent. Includes both the elevation grid and the Type Identifier (TID) grid documenting the source/quality of each cell.
