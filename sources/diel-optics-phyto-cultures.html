Bio-optical Properties of Cultured Phytoplankton Over Complete Diel Cycles

Laboratory culture study measuring bio-optical and physiological properties (absorption spectra, pigment composition, particulate organic carbon, growth rates) of phytoplankton cultures sampled across complete diel (24-hour) cycles, supporting a manuscript on diel variability in phytoplankton optics.
