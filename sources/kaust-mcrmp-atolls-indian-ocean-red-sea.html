Atolls of the Indian Ocean and Red Sea - Geomorphological Vector Data (MCRMP)

Shapefile-based geomorphological maps of coral reef atolls across the Indian Ocean and Red Sea region, from the Millennium Coral Reef Mapping Project (MCRMP), created by photo-interpretation of Landsat 7/8 imagery (Andrefouet, 2023). Distributed as per-region zipped shapefile bundles; only the Sudan bundle falls within the Red Sea bounding box, the remainder (BIOT/Chagos, Lakshadweep, Maldives, Seychelles) cover other Indian Ocean atoll systems but are archived together in this KAUST folder.
