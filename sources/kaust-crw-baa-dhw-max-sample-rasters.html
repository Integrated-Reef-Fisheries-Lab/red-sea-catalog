NOAA Coral Reef Watch 5km BAA-max & DHW-max Sample Monthly Rasters

Two standalone monthly NOAA Coral Reef Watch (CRW) 5km satellite coral bleaching heat stress rasters found loose in the Environmental folder root: a Bleaching Alert Area maximum (BAA-max) composite for April 1985 and a Degree Heating Week maximum (DHW-max) composite for January 2023. These appear to be single-month sample/test downloads of the standard CRW v3.1 product suite rather than a continuous archived time series.
