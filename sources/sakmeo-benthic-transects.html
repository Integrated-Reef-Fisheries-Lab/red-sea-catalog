SAKMEO Benthic Photo-Quadrat Cover — Point-Count Transects (2014-2023)

Wide-format benthic cover point-count data from SAKMEO (Saudi Aramco-KAUST Marine Environmental Observatory) photo-quadrat surveys, covering hundreds of transects across reef sites from Duba in the north to Farasan-adjacent sites, surveyed intermittently between 2014 and 2023 (repeat visits at some sites in 2014, 2016, 2017, 2019, and 2023). Each row is a benthic cover category (e.g. hard coral, macroalgae, rubble, sand, debris); each column is one transect (site + year + replicate), with cover values as proportions. A companion Metadata sheet gives per-transect site name, coordinates, region, reef, depth, exposure, and distance-to-shore.
