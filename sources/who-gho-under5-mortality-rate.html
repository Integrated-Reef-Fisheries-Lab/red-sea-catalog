WHO Global Health Observatory — Under-Five Mortality Rate (All Countries, 1950-Present)

WHO Global Health Observatory (GHO) export of the under-five mortality rate indicator (MDG_0000000007: probability of dying by age 5, per 1,000 live births), by country, sex, and year, for all UN member states from 1950 to the most recent estimate year, with upper/lower 95% uncertainty bounds.

Known issues: Filename (2322814_ALL_LATEST.csv) is a raw GHO export identifier; indicator identity (under-five mortality rate, code MDG_0000000007) was inferred from the IND_NAME/IND_CODE columns during cataloging and was not independently cross-checked against the live WHO GHO catalog.; Global, all-country dataset with only a single indicator; only the Saudi Arabia rows (M49 code 682) are relevant to this catalog's Red Sea scope, but the full global file is retained locally.; Included as a health-outcome indicator plausibly linked to nutritional status (as referenced by the team's aspirational SEDAC/infant-mortality entry in Sources - NH.Rmd), not a direct nutrition or seafood-consumption measure.; No sub-national spatial detail; national Saudi Arabia figures applied uniformly across all five subbasins.
