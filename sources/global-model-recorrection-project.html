Global Model Recorrection Project — Site-Level Chlorophyll, SST Climatology & NPP Extractions

Working dataset for a project recalibrating a global chlorophyll/productivity model, comprising site-level chlorophyll time series extracted at ~2,000 reef survey locations (from satellite chlorophyll products, at both native and 5 km resampled resolution), monthly Coral Reef Watch composites, NOAA OI SST monthly climatologies (1981-2010 and 1991-2020 baselines), and net primary productivity (NPP) outputs, all indexed to the same site list.
