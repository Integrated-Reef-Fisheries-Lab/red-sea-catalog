Red Sea Combined Fish and Benthic Transect Survey Data (2008-2011)

Combined fish and benthic transect survey dataset covering northern Red Sea reef sites around Al Wajh Bank (including sites named 'Wajh' and 'Screw Loose 1/2/3'). Contains two parallel worksheets -- a raw transect-by-taxon sheet and a zero-filled version (absent taxa recorded as zero rather than omitted) -- plus a site/locations lookup sheet. Covers reef fish counts and benthic cover/functional-group categories (corals, algae, sponges, substrate types).
