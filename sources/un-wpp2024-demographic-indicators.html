UN World Population Prospects 2024 — General Demographic Indicators (F01)

United Nations World Population Prospects 2024 revision, General file F01 'Demographic Indicators' — annual population, fertility, mortality, and migration indicators for all countries and regions of the world, 1950-2023 (Estimates sheet), with additional projection-variant sheets to 2100. Includes Saudi Arabia as one of ~300 geographic entities; not Red-Sea or coastal-specific.
