National Health Accounts (NHA) Indicators — Saudi Arabia

Health financing and expenditure indicators for Saudi Arabia (e.g. Current Health Expenditure as % of GDP, per-capita health expenditure, expenditure by financing scheme, health care function, and provider type), reported annually from 2000 onward, following the WHO/OECD/Eurostat System of Health Accounts framework.
