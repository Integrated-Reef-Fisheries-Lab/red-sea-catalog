ReefScale Global Reef Fish Biomass and Management Dataset

Global compilation of reef fish biomass survey data (belt-transect visual census) linked to socio-economic gravity metrics (travel time to market/population, reserve status, management type) at thousands of coral reef sites worldwide, used for cross-regional benchmarking of Red Sea reef fish biomass against global reef condition gradients.
